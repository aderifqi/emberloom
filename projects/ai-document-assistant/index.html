Project Snapshot

Search, retrieval, and answer composition wrapped in a simple product flow designed for business users, not technical operators.

Problem Fit

Teams often store knowledge across scattered PDFs, documents, and internal references. The product concept reduces the time spent opening files one by one, while keeping answers tied to the original source context.

User NeedFind reliable answers from internal documentation without manual reading across multiple files.
Design GoalKeep the experience simple enough for operations, finance, and support teams to use daily.

What’s Included

Upload FlowDummy upload area for bringing in business documents and processing them into a searchable workspace.
Answer UIConversation layout with source-linked responses and visible traceability.
Admin LayerBasic management concept for document collections, roles, and content freshness.

Tools & Stack
