Project Snapshot

Consumer finance concept focused on clarity over feature overload, with a cleaner dashboard and easier money tracking habits.

User Focus

The experience is shaped around users who need instant visibility into spending and balances, but do not want dense financial software. The interface direction favors calm visuals, obvious hierarchy, and short decision loops.

Main NeedTrack spending and monitor habits from one simple mobile dashboard.
UX DirectionLower visual noise, use plain-language categories, and keep analytics lightweight.

Dummy Scope
